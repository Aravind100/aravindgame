Game Rules:

1.You have to select number in between 1-10,
no less than 1 and no greater 10.

2.Your total should be 100 or more to win the game.
3.If you get 100 or more before computer,
you will win the match.

For example:
if
on same round
your total is = 98
and computer's total is = 100 or more
you lose the game.

if
on same round
your total is = 100 or more before computer
you win the game.
